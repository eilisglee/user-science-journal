Usability Test on Postable.com

Postable.com

Posted by Eilis Lee on April 26, 2020

Understanding how people interact with a website is essential to designing a good product. For today's article, we'll take a look at Postable's website, www.postable.com. Postable allows its users to create custom greeting cards and mails them. These are not eCards--they're good ol' fashioned paper greeting cards.

Postable.com Homepage

The main goal of this study was to evaluate how effective the current menu headings are in allowing users to find the information they are searching for. The study took place virtually over Zoom and the participants were given a number of tasks to complete.

Methodology

What participants did

Each of the participants were met with separately and completed a total of 3 tasks which involved searching the site in order to complete them. The sessions lasted between 15-30 minutes.

What data was collected

The data collected included the paths taken, the time spent on each task, and the task completion rate.

Detailed findings and recommendations


Task 1 - Find and purchase a greeting card

Your mother's birthday is coming up and you would like to send her a greeting card. Browse the greeting card offerings and purchase one for her.

  • Number of Participants: 3
  • Percent Successful: 100%
Findings Recommendations
All participants completed the task with ease by finding the birthday card offerings and adding the item to cart. Add a feature to allow user to filter cards by recipient.

The participants were able to navigate successfully within the application to locate the birthday card section. Once in the birthday card section, the majority of the time spent on this task was in searching for a suitable card. Once found, the participants were able to quickly customize, address, and add the cards to their cart.

Postable dropdown menu for greeting card offerings

Task 2 - Find information on scheduling

You're interested in learning about how you can schedule multiple birthday cards for the entire year. Find where you can learn more about scheduling.

  • Number of Participants: 3
  • Percent Successful: 67%
Findings Recommendations
2 of the 3 participants completed the task with ease by finding the mass mailing section and clicking on the Learn more link under Automated Birthday Cards. Make the Learn more link more visually obvious.

Two of the three participants were able to find the information under Learn more with very little difficulty. The third participant did not complete the task, and instead gave up after determining that the more visually obvious link, Start automation, did not provide the information needed. The Start automation link opens up to a sign-up page and does not include any information about scheduling future mailings.

Automate birthday cards

Task 3 - Find Pricing

You're interested in sending holiday greeting cards to all of your customers this year. Locate pricing on volume business greeting cards.

  • Number of Participants: 3
  • Percent Successful: 100%
Findings Recommendations
All participants were able to successfully complete the task of finding pricing on business greeting cards. Make the pricing calculator stand out more.

The participants were able to find their way to the pricing calculator with little difficulty. Clicking on the See our pricing link under the Business heading sets the position of the page to the Pricing Calculator, which includes the rates per card or postcard.

Volume pricing on business cards

Clicking in the textbox above the "Calculate" button allows people to input their own numbers but this field can be easily missed as the color is not very obvious from the background of the page. Only 2 of the 3 participants noticed this field.
